## 🧠 About Me

I'm a Senior AI/ML Software Engineer with 3+ years of experience bridging the gap between **cutting-edge AI systems** and **hardware verification**. I architect production-grade RAG pipelines, agentic AI systems, and cloud-native distributed platforms — while also being deeply versed in ASIC physical design, formal verification, and RTL engineering.

Currently building [**Relyra AI**](https://github.com/Tharun007-hyphens/relyra-AI-showcase) — a RAG-powered agentic AI platform designed to automate and accelerate ASIC/FPGA formal verification workflows.

- 🤖 Building production RAG & Agentic AI systems for chip design verification
- ⚡ Experienced in ASIC physical design from RTL to GDSII (Yosys + OpenROAD)
- ☁️ Shipping cloud-native distributed systems on AWS/Azure with Kafka & Kubernetes
- 🎓 MS Computer Engineering, University of Florida — hardware + AI intersection
